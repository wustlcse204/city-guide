PLACES TO EAT

RESTAURANT OF THE DAY:
DUKEM ETHIOPIAN RESTAURANT

Dukem Ethiopian Restaurant is the most enduring Ethiopian music entertainment landmark in the district. The newly expanded VIP BAR can serve you with full bar for your private use for a number of Celebrations including Birthday Parties, Graduation party and private events, with the finest Ethiopian music in town and famous singers DC has to offer. Over the past twelve years, Dukem's entertainment has reached fame both in DC and Nationally. Dukem's has been selected as best Ethiopian restaurant by Washington City Paper Readers Poll 2008, 2009, 2010 & 2011. Dukem's provides a large variety of vegetarian and non vegetarian meals, with the choice to make your food Mild, Medium or Hot. Check out all of their mouth-watering cuisine Dukem has to offer for Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner!

HOURS 11AM-2AM
